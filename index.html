Frequently Asked Questions
What is Coding CLub

A environement where students can learn 'Computational thinking' and principles of computer science. Learning these things with help of friends, seniors and students.

What do Students will do at coding club?

Coding club will give students a opportunity to build projects with each others help. As well as to learn things from tutorials and programs made by others. To innovate things that will actually cause a change to this world.

I have never code can I join?

Even if you don't have any knowledge you can join us. Ask teachers, friends and can make your way out for this. Coding club will try to guide you on each stage.
